What Size Liner do I Use for my Litterbox?

This calculator is the best way of determining what size liner you should use for your litterbox.
We take the dimensions of your litterbox and match the size of your litterbox with the appropriate AlfaPet liner. To do this, you must enter your litterbox's length, width, and heights in inches. Please use the pictures below for any questions you may have about properly measuring your litterbox.

*All measurements must be in INCHES

Cat Pan Liner Calculator




To measure the dimensions of your litterbox, you must identify the values of the length, width, front, back, left, and right sides. The front, back, left and right sides are HEIGHT values.
